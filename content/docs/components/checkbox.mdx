---
title: Checkbox
description: SketchCheckbox — a hand-drawn checkbox with a spring-animated tick mark.
---

`SketchCheckbox` renders a rough.js box and an animated check mark. It works
uncontrolled out of the box — pass both `checked` and `onChange` to take full
control.

## Default

<ComponentPreview
  code={`import { SketchCheckbox } from "blackchalk";

<SketchCheckbox />
<SketchCheckbox checked={true} />`}
>
  <SketchCheckbox />
  <SketchCheckbox checked={true} />
