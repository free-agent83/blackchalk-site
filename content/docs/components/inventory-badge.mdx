---
title: Inventory Badge
description: SketchInventoryBadge — a hand-drawn badge for displaying product stock status.
---

`SketchInventoryBadge` renders a rough.js badge labelled with a stock state — in
stock, low stock, or out of stock — switching its visual treatment automatically
based on the `status` prop.

## Default

<ComponentPreview
  code={`import { SketchInventoryBadge } from "blackchalk";

<SketchInventoryBadge status="in-stock" />
<SketchInventoryBadge status="low-stock" />
<SketchInventoryBadge status="out-of-stock" />`}
>
  <SketchInventoryBadge status="in-stock" />
  <SketchInventoryBadge status="low-stock" />
  <SketchInventoryBadge status="out-of-stock" />
</ComponentPreview>

## Props

| Prop     | Type                                              | Default |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ------- |
| `status` | `"in-stock" \| "low-stock" \| "out-of-stock"` | —       |
